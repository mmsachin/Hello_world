## How I use AI in my work

I treat AI as a force multiplier, not a replacement for understanding
the system. A few patterns that have worked for me:

- **Spec before prompt.** I write a short design doc or acceptance
  criteria first, then hand it to the model. Better input, better output.
- **Code review partner.** I run diffs through [tool] before sending for
  human review — catches obvious issues so reviewers can focus on design.
- **Doc drafting.** First drafts of runbooks, postmortems, and design
  docs. I always rewrite for voice and verify claims.
- **Test generation.** Especially for edge cases I'd otherwise miss.
- **Learning unfamiliar code.** Paste in a module, ask for a tour.

What I don't use AI for: anything that ships without me reading every
line, anything touching prod credentials, or decisions that need
business context the model doesn't have.
